About Jeff

Jeff Rendel is based in the heart of Saint Louis, Missouri, specializing in personal injury law. With a passion for justice and a commitment to his clients, Jeff brings extensive legal expertise and a compassionate approach to every case he handles.

A Saint Louis native, Mr. Rendel graduated from Beaumont in 1994, and earned a Bachelor degree from the University of North Carolina in 1998. He returned to his hometown to attend the Washington University School of Law, where he earned his Juris Doctor degree in 2001.

Throughout his career, Jeff has successfully represented countless clients in a variety of personal injury cases, including car accidents, slip and fall incidents, medical malpractice, and wrongful death claims. His in-depth knowledge of personal injury law, coupled with his tenacity in the courtroom, has led to numerous favorable outcomes for his clients, earning him a reputation as a trusted advocate in the Saint Louis legal community.

Beyond his legal practice, Jeff is deeply committed to giving back to his community. He volunteers his time with local organizations and frequently participates in pro bono work to ensure that everyone has access to quality legal representation, regardless of their financial circumstances.

Jeff understands that navigating the legal system can be overwhelming, especially for those dealing with the aftermath of a serious injury. That's why he takes a hands-on approach to every case, providing personalized attention and guidance to his clients at every step of the legal process. He believes in building strong, trusting relationships with those he represents, and he works tirelessly to achieve the best possible results on their behalf.

When he's not in the office or the courtroom, Jeff enjoys spending time with his family, exploring the vibrant cultural scene of Saint Louis, and staying active in the local community.

If you've been injured and are seeking experienced legal representation you can trust, contact Jeff Rendel today to schedule a consultation. He's here to fight for your rights and help you get the justice and compensation you deserve.
